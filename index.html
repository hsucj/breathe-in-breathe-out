Breathe In, Breathe Out

Rethinking the CPS Experience @ Princeton

Annie Chen, Chris Hsu, Cindy Liu, and Rani Jaiswal

The Project


The process of booking CPS appointments is riddled with frustration for many students on campus. Our goal is to redesign the interface of CPS to create a system that encourages, rather than deters, students from reaching out to someone in times of emotional distress. We aim to do this by reducing the number of steps involved in the system, using a “less is more” approach when presenting information, and creating a more empathetic and calming design.
